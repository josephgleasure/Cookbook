Robuchon Potato Puree - Mashed Potatoes

Serves 6Prep 15 minutesCook 35 minutes
Home

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes, preferably rattes or BF 15, scrubbed and unpeeled
  • Coarse salt
  • 250 g butter, diced and well chilled
  • 250 ml whole milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Put potatoes in a saucepan with 2 liters cold water and 1 tablespoon coarse salt. Bring to a simmer, cover, cook until a knife slips in easily, about 25 minutes.
  2. Drain and peel potatoes. Rice them into a large saucepan using a ricer or a fine disk on a food mill.
  3. Set pan over medium heat. Dry the potato by turning vigorously with a spatula for about 5 minutes.
  4. Rinse a small saucepan, leave slightly wet, add milk, bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat under potatoes to low. Incorporate well chilled butter bit by bit, stirring energetically until smooth.
  6. Pour in very hot milk in a thin stream, still over low heat, stirring briskly until absorbed. Taste and adjust salt and pepper.
  7. Optional for extra fine texture: pass the puree through a very fine sieve before serving.

Tip: salt the cooking water while cold. Salt the finished puree carefully. Avoid blenders or food processors.
